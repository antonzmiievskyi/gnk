FAQ

How quickly can I start participating?
Provisioning typically completes within days after KYC verification and contract signing. We provide a clear KYC checklist upfront to avoid delays.
Can I confirm infrastructure fit before committing budget?
Yes. We provide detailed spec sheets and confirm architecture requirements (network nodes, GPU servers, managed Kubernetes, IPs, regions) before you sign a contract.
What happens if servers are down?
We continuously monitor infrastructure. If downtime is caused by our equipment or network, we compensate non-working hours according to SLA and adjust invoices accordingly.
How does billing work?
  • Month-to-month contracts
  • Payment for first month required before server assignment
  • Renewal: Pay no later than 10 days before contract end
  • Monthly invoices sent at calendar month end
  • Transparent ledger shows all charges and balance movements
Can I scale up or down?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. We provide fast quote turnaround and clear price/volume agreements for scaling plans.
What support is available?
Mon–Fri business hours support. No weekend onboarding. Support responds to infrastructure issues and provides clear next steps.
Are there discounts for longer commitments?
Yes. Discounts available with 3+ months commitment.
What monitoring and visibility do I get?
Access to performance statistics. Automated dashboard with real-time statistics, GPU performance, and network activity (coming soon). Telegram bot monitoring planned.
How are payments handled?
Payment cadence can be negotiated (split advances, multi-payer flows) to support scaling plans. Card payment limits can be accommodated through alternative arrangements.
What if I need to change my setup?
Contact support to discuss architecture changes, region adjustments, or capacity modifications. We confirm availability and provide updated quotes before changes.
